Welcome to the 62Forward
Mandate Portal

As part of our commitment to responsive and inclusive governance, we have set up the 62Forward Mandate Portal to mobilze ward-level solutions to the varied challenges faced across communities in the FCT.

We have enumerated 20 demand action points as articulated by the Nigerian people and seek to uphold democratic principles of ensuring that every voice is heard and concerns are addressed in the most responsive ways, to carve a shared path forward.

Complex policy challenges cannot be tackled by the government alone and achieving high-quality public services and shared policy goals requires moving beyond a 'business-as-usual' approach and more active citizen involvement.

Thank you for choosing to play your part in helping us further our commitment to inclusive and responsive governance.
